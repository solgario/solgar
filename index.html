How it works

🎯
Goal
Grow the biggest, highest-mass cell and climb the leaderboard. You gain mass by eating — the longer you survive, the bigger you get.
🪙
Getting in
Enter a name, connect your Solana wallet (proves it's yours and is where rewards go), pick a skin, and hit PLAY. Live play needs 250,000 $SOLGAR in that wallet — or spectate free without tokens.
💰
Win SOL
Every 10 minutes the round ends and the top 5 cells split creator-fee SOL, paid straight to their wallets. Rewards go live at mint.
🟢
Eat to grow
Swallow the glowing orbs across the map. Gold orbs are worth +5 each — well worth chasing down.
⚔️
Eat rivals
Cells 25% bigger than another can swallow it whole. Pop a rival and you absorb them — their leftovers scatter as loot for everyone.
🎮
Controls
Steer with the joystick on mobile or the mouse on desktop. Space splits your cell, F ejects mass to feed teammates or viruses.
🧱
The edge is lethal
The glowing red boundary kills on contact — don't let anyone corner you into it.
🛡️
Spawn shield
You're briefly invincible (cyan ring) right after spawning, so you won't get instantly eaten in a crowd.
👑
Crown & resets
The biggest cell on screen wears a 👑. The whole arena wipes clean for fresh competition every round.
